"A cosmos is a good place to do category theory."

Ramblings in category theory, at CMUC. Starting in September.

The setting

 We're joining together to teach and learn category theory in a friendly environment. You are not supposed to already master category theory, but it's essential that you already like it (if not, join us and we will convince you). :-)

 Everyone is welcome to contribute by attending the lectures, giving a seminar, adding an item to our list of topics,...
 We set up a doodle poll where you can manifest your interest on the project: tick "Attend" if you just like the idea of passing by and listen; tick "present" if you would like to prepare a lecture.

 All the meetings will be held at the CMUC in Coimbra; further details about the room and schedule will be added here in due time.
